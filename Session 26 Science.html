The Science Behind Covid

Ever since the Covid outbreak, the world's top scientists have been working hard non-stop to find out more about this terrible virus. They have made many major breakthroughs which have lead up to the creation of the vaccine. At the moment, there are two Covid vaccines. One is called the Astra-Zenica vaine and the other is called the Pfizer vaccine. The Astra-Zenica vaccine works like a normal vaccine. It injects a weak version of Covid into you so your body can create an antidote. The Pfizer is a more modern vaccinne and works in a more complex way.
